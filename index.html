Method
AI model DINOv3 (ConvNeXt-L) Siamese change-detection, with building footprints refined by Overture.
Trained on Pre- and post-event optical imagery from three previous earthquakes:
Mexico (2017)Morocco (2023)Türkiye–Syria (2023)
Zero-shot validation Evaluated on 2,244 buildings graded by Copernicus EMS

Of the buildings Copernicus reported as destroyed, the model predicted 74.2% as damaged or destroyed, and 68.5% exactly as destroyed.
